How do I play?
Tap the colorful circle as fast as you can before time runs out. Build combos to increase your multiplier!

What are power-ups?
Green power-ups help you (extra lives, slow time, double points). Red power-ups are bad - avoid them!

How do I unlock new levels?
Collect points by playing rounds. Each level requires more points to unlock. Check the Level Select menu to see your progress.

Can I reset my progress?
Yes! Use the Reset button in the main menu to reset your highscores or level progress.

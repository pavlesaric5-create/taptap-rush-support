Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2025

TapTap Rush is committed to protecting your privacy. This game is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

TapTap Rush does NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any information that could identify you

Data Storage

The only data stored by TapTap Rush is:

  • Game Progress: Your level progress, points, and unlocked levels
  • Highscores: Your personal Top 3 scores
  • Settings: Sound and haptic feedback preferences

This data is stored locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults. It never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

TapTap Rush does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The game operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

TapTap Rush is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children using this app.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and is protected by iOS security features. When you delete the app, all locally stored data is automatically removed.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Reset Progress: Use the Reset button in the game menu to delete your level progress
  • Reset Highscores: Use the Reset button to delete your saved scores
  • Complete Removal: Deleting the app removes all data permanently

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

App Permissions

TapTap Rush does not request any special iOS permissions. The app only uses:

  • Haptic Feedback: Optional haptic feedback when tapping (can be disabled in settings)
  • Local Storage: To save your game progress and scores on your device
